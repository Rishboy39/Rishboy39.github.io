About me

Hey, I'm Rishabh Menasinakayi, a sophomore at Tesla STEM High School in Redmond, WA. Originally from India, I'm passionate about Engineering, Computer Science, and the exciting possibilities of artificial intelligence (AI). On the soccer field, I thrive on teamwork and strategy, while off the field, I'm captivated by the inner workings of cars. Coding is my creative playground, especially in web development. Beyond that, I cherish family time and enjoy gaming with Fortnite and Minecraft.

As I look to the future, I'm particularly intrigued by the potential integration of AI in automotive technology. Imagining a world where AI enhances vehicle safety, efficiency, and the overall driving experience excites me. As I pursue my academic journey, I'm eager to explore the intersections of AI and engineering, working towards a career that combines my passions and drives innovation. This website is a snapshot of my journey, capturing my growth, interests, and aspirations as I chart a course towards a future where technology and humanity intersect.
